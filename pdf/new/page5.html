WHEN YOU FEEL OPEN TO NEW POSSIBILITIES THEY
BEGIN TO SHOW THEMSELVES.
For me, I had to give my son ownership and let him say 'No' if he wanted
without reprisal. He began to trust me and more meaningful conversations
followed by his initiation.
MY TOP 3 LIGHTS ON® TAKE AWAYS
1. Feel Open – When we are frustrated and feeling like nothing is working we
are probably in quite a closed state of mind and looking at what is not
happening. Feeling open to the process is about looking for positives and
working with them. Positives are there but probably not where you have been
looking. When you feel open to new possibilities they begin to show
themselves.
2. Be Brave – Your child’s switch may not be within your comfort zone. Try
not to nudge children into your way of doing things but look for the way in
which they do things.
3. Terminology is important. Think about how you phrase things. Changing
one word can make a huge difference. You are going for more of an
assistant/collaborator role. Do you think you come across more like the
leader/teacher? This may be blocking your progress. Shifting dynamics
within your parent child relationship is difficult and needs to be thought
about and practised. What works for your child?
