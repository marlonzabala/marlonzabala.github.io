I NEED TO SPEND TIME WITH MY CHILDREN AND
PROPERLY CONNECT
As a parent I've had to be brave. I've encouraged my son to do the Lights On®
activities not knowing how he'd react to the ideas and when he did them how
he'd get on. After watching some of the resources in the membership portal
about mindset it reminded me how my son has taken me from a fixed mindset
to a growth one. Our kids can teach us so much!
MY TOP 3 LIGHTS ON® TAKE AWAYS
1. Timing plays a huge part, especially with my teenager. I need to spend
time with my children and properly connect before they are fully receptive to
discussions about moving their passions forward.
2. Space has played a big part in identifying the passions in our house -
creating down time where I could watch what drew their interest.
3. Enabling is key as well. When my daughter wanted to pursue her singing
we had to find the right teacher who was open to her leading part of her
learning. With my son's interest in filmmaking, we needed to support him in
finding the right apps and technology.
